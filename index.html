Welcome to Guns and Hoses

We offer equipment, cleaning services, uniforms, merchandise, and so much more!

Info

Guns and Hoses is located in Lake Foest, California and is proud to serve any & all first responders in order to meet their wants/needs to help keep them serving safely and swiftly.

Meet the CEO

Our business not only serves the protecting members of our community, but we serve in the same occupations as our customers as well. Our founder, Joseph Kerr is on his way to becoming a firefighter in the OCFA (Orange County Fire Authority). He started Guns and Hoses earlier this year in the hopes that his concerns and the concerns of those he works with are met with an appropiate response in the form of our merchandise and services.

Products & Services

We offer a variety of products and services to meet your everyday needs!

Knowledgeable Staff

All of our staff are not only trained in the professions of first responders, but they are constantly working to learn the wants/needs of our customers to better serve those who serve our communities.

Convenient Location

We are located in the middle of OCFA Divisions 3, 4, & 5 and in between 3 local OCSD (Orange County Sheriff's Department) locations in order to offer convenience to our customers and speedy service for when you are in a pinch.

24/7 Customer Support

We know how tough it can be out there, especially when you receive late night dispatches to expanded traffic colissions, commercial fires, etc. which is why we offer 24/7 services to those in need of custom sized equipment/tools and new uniforms at a moments notice.

Recycling

Our products are made from recycled materials in an effort to help our environment, and to take it a step further, if you bring in your old eqiupment for replacement we give a discount on your next purchase based on the weight and condition of the item received.
